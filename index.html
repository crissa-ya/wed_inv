God's Perfect Timing
“A Love Story”
“He has made everything beautiful in its time.”
— Ecclesiastes 3:11
Every beautiful story draws its first breath from God's perfect timing, and ours is no exception. This is the story of two lives, two hearts, and one faithful God who wrote our names on the same page long before we ever knew the other existed.
Long before we knew each other's names, the Lord was weaving our separate lives into a single tapestry of grace. We were each other's first love, young and hopeful, unaware of the journey ahead.
God tenderly shaped our hearts through seasons of abundance and silence, gently loosening our grip on our own timelines. He was preparing us for seven years of refining that would test everything we believed about love, faith, and surrender.
For seven wonderful years, our love was tested by distance. Though miles often separated us, our hearts never did. Each parting was a sacred promise, veiled in tears, sealed in prayer. When our strength failed, God was nearest. He held us when we could not hold each other. We learned that faithfulness is a choice made new every morning.
Those years of being apart taught us this eternal truth: No ocean, no border, no earthly divide can sever what God has united in love. The miles between us were not barriers, but altars where we laid down our fears and trusted God. Distance did not diminish our love, it refined it.
And then, in the fullness of His appointed time, neither early nor late, He answered. The waiting yielded its harvest. The dreams we had long carried in quiet prayer became the reality we now cherish with open hands. The miles between us closed. The promises we had whispered into the dark came forth into the light. God's perfect timing had arrived. Not a moment too soon. Not a moment too late. Precisely when it was meant to be.
The years of distance were not wasted. They were the soil in which our love grew deep roots, roots that would withstand any storm, any season, any trial. And when the answer finally came, it was not just an answer to our prayers, it was a testimony to God's faithfulness. A reminder that He who began a good work in us would carry it on to completion.
Today, with hearts humbled and spirits overflowing with gratitude, we stand upon the threshold of forever. We do not step forward as those who have arrived, but as those who are still being carried by the same faithful Hand that led us here. We enter this sacred new chapter not trusting in our own strength, but resting wholly in the One who has borne us through every mile, every midnight, and every dawn. We look back and see His fingerprints on every page of our story, the moments of joy, the seasons of sorrow, the years of waiting, and now, this moment of fulfillment. We look forward and see not a perfect future, but a future held by a perfect God. We are not perfect people. But we serve a perfect God who makes all things beautiful in His time. And so, we stand here, not as two individuals who found each other, but as two souls who were found by the same loving Father, brought together by His grace, and sustained by His faithfulness.
And now, we invite you, our beloved family and friends, to witness the next chapter of our story. You have been part of this journey. You have prayed for us, encouraged us, and believed with us. You have seen us through the distance and celebrated with us in the reunions. You have been the hands and feet of God's love in our lives. And so, it is with overflowing joy that we ask you to join us as we exchange vows and begin our forever.
