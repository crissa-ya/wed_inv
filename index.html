Our Love Story

Every beautiful story begins with God's perfect timing, and ours is no exception.

What began as two separate journeys became one path filled with faith, patience, and love. Through every season, God prepared our hearts and taught us to trust His perfect plan.

For seven wonderful years, our love was tested by distance. Though miles often separated us, our hearts never did. Every goodbye became a promise to meet again, every challenge strengthened our faith, and every season drew us closer together. We became each other's comfort, choosing faithfulness, understanding, and love every single day. Those seven years proved that no distance is greater than a love anchored in God.

In His perfect time, our prayers were answered, and our dreams became reality.

Today, with grateful hearts, we begin the greatest chapter of our story, trusting the One who has faithfully guided us every step of the way.

This is only the beginning of our forever.
